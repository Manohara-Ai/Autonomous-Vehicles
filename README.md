# Autonomous Vehicle Innovation Hub

**A hub for brainstorming innovative solutions to autonomous vehicle challenges. Focused on ideas and simulations!**

---

## Purpose
To explore and address key challenges in autonomous vehicle technology by:
- Brainstorming innovative ideas.
- Developing and testing solutions through simulations.

---

## Focus Areas
1. **Pedestrian and Cyclist Safety**  
   Enhancing detection and avoidance systems for improved road safety.

2. **Traffic Flow Optimization**  
   Implementing real-time communication systems to manage urban traffic efficiently.

3. **Energy Efficiency**  
   Developing eco-friendly routing algorithms for autonomous electric vehicles.

4. **Communication and Coordination**  
   Improving Vehicle-to-Vehicle (V2V) and Vehicle-to-Infrastructure (V2I) protocols.

5. **Simulations**  
   Testing ideas and solutions in virtual environments before real-world implementation.

---

## Current Projects
- **Dynamic HD Mapping**  
  Collaborative mapping using data from multiple autonomous vehicles.

- **Behavior Prediction Models**  
  AI-powered systems to predict the actions of pedestrians and other vehicles.

- **Eco-Friendly Routing**  
  Optimizing energy consumption for electric autonomous vehicles.

1. Share your ideas on innovative solutions.
2. Participate in simulation development and testing.
3. Collaborate on research and development projects.
